{# IRC Chat #}

IRC Chat

If the above two methods do not work for you, IRC Chat is the fastest way you can get your answers. Just install an IRC Chat client (we recommend Pidgin and XChat) and join our real-time discussion at #dingo on irc.gnome.org. If there is anyone available they will immediately reply to your questions.
