{# Playback #}

Playback

Music
Dingo is able to recognize, import and play many different audio formats (mp3, aac, ogg, etc...) thanks to its GStreamer-based engine.
Videos
Dingo is able to recognize, import and play many different video formats (avi, mpeg, mkv, etc...) thanks to its GStreamer-based engine.
Fullscreen
Expand your videos to fullscreen to maximize your experience.
Subtitle
Improve your movie-watching experience using our subtitle engine: import subtitle, autosave subtitle and choose its format
Repeat Modes
Dingo has four repeat modes that can suit all of your needs: Repeat Off, Repeat Playlist, Repeat Single, Repeat Section (our special and unique one!).
Edit Lyrics
Dingo supports fetching and saving lyrics to files. You will no longer have to open your browser for lyrics-lookup while listening to your favorite pieces.
